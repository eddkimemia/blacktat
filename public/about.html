Black Maple Ink exists to prove that permanent body art can meet the same bar as fine art: intention, craftsmanship, hygiene, and hospitality.

From Queen West to a National Network

Black Maple Ink was founded on a single conviction: tattoo artistry belongs at the level of fine art. Every piece should be as singular as the person who wears it — precise, personal, and permanent.

What began as a Queen West Toronto atelier is now a multi-city network of luxury studios in Toronto, Vancouver, and Calgary — unified by the same standards of hygiene, hospitality, and craft. We never franchise quality; we replicate discipline.

We book online before you visit. Your deposit is secured with Paystack. Your suite is prepared. Your artist already knows your vision. That is the Black Maple difference.

Principles

What We Stand For

Art over volume

We limit daily bookings so each composition receives the hours it deserves. Your session is never a conveyor-belt appointment.

Radical transparency

Clear deposits, written estimates, named artists, and documented hygiene. You always know who is working on you and what you are paying for.

Respect for permanence

We design for how ink lives in skin over decades — not just how it photographs on day one. Longevity is part of the craft.

Questions and answers

Pain levels vary by placement and personal tolerance. Most clients describe a scratching or vibrating sensation. Private suites, experienced artists, and optional numbing (discussed in consultation) help minimize discomfort.

Pricing depends on size, complexity, placement, and artist. Minimum is typically $150 CAD. Transparent quotes are given during consultation — no hidden add-ons. Starting ranges are listed on our Services page.

You select a studio, date, and time online, then pay a non-refundable deposit through Paystack’s secure checkout. Your appointment is confirmed only after successful payment — before you visit the studio. The deposit applies to your final balance. We never store full card numbers.

We require 48 hours notice for cancellations or rescheduling. Less notice forfeits the deposit. No-shows may be charged the session minimum. This policy protects artists’ blocked time and clients who are waiting for slots.

We primarily work by appointment so each client receives dedicated private-suite time. Booking and paying your deposit online is the best way to secure your preferred artist and guarantee a prepared room.

Stay hydrated, eat a solid meal, avoid alcohol and blood-thinning substances for 24 hours when medically appropriate, and wear clothing that allows access to the tattoo area. We send a prep guide after confirmed booking. Bring government ID (18+ for tattoos).

Every studio uses single-use needles and tubes, medical-grade barriers, surface disinfection between clients, and autoclave sterilization for reusable tools. Private suites reduce cross-traffic. You are welcome to ask any artist about the workflow before your session begins.

Yes. Browse artists by specialty and studio city, then book the specialist whose portfolio matches your vision. Many clients travel between Toronto, Vancouver, and Calgary specifically for a named artist.

You leave with written aftercare instructions and product guidance. General principles: keep the area clean, avoid soaking (pools, baths, ocean) during early healing, don’t pick scabs, and protect from sun once healed. Follow your artist’s specific sheet — styles and placements can differ.

Yes. Cover-ups require consultation photos and realistic design planning. Touch-ups for work done at Black Maple Ink are discussed case-by-case during healing follow-up. Always book through the online system so records stay attached to your deposit reference.

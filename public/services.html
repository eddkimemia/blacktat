Transparent Ranges. No Guesswork.

Final pricing depends on size, complexity, placement, and artist. The ranges on this page are starting points. During consultation you receive a clear estimate before any needle touches skin. Your online deposit applies to the total.

  • ✓Studio minimum typically starts around $150 CAD
  • ✓Deposit required online to lock the appointment
  • ✓Large projects may be planned across multiple sessions
  • ✓Cover-ups and realism often require extra design time

Why deposit first?

A deposit protects your artist’s blocked time, funds custom design hours, and guarantees your private suite is prepared. It is not an extra fee — it is applied to your final balance after the session.

Payments are processed securely through Paystack. We never store full card numbers on our servers.

Questions & Answers

Frequently Asked

Pain levels vary by placement and personal tolerance. Most clients describe a scratching sensation. Private suites, comfortable seating, and experienced artists help minimize discomfort. Numbing options can be discussed during your consultation.

Pricing depends on size, complexity, placement, and artist. Our minimum is $150 CAD. Small pieces typically $150–$250, medium $300–$600, large custom work from $800+. Transparent quotes during consultation — no surprises.

Absolutely. We welcome reference images and your own concepts. Our artists refine and adapt your idea so it translates beautifully to skin. Every tattoo is a collaboration.

A $50 CAD non-refundable deposit secures your appointment and applies to the final cost. Reschedule with at least 48 hours notice to transfer your deposit.

Stay hydrated, eat well before your session, avoid alcohol for 24 hours, and wear clothing that allows easy access to the tattoo area. We'll send a detailed prep guide after booking.

We require 48 hours notice for cancellations or rescheduling. Less notice forfeits the deposit. No-shows are charged the session minimum. Life happens — communicate early and we'll help.

We primarily work by appointment so each client receives dedicated time. Walk-ins are accommodated when availability allows. Booking in advance ensures your preferred artist.
